What do you think success means?

Success is a subjective concept that can have different meanings for different individuals. Generally, success is often associated with the achievement of goals, fulfillment, and a sense of accomplishment in various areas of life. It goes beyond mere financial wealth or social status and encompasses personal satisfaction, happiness, and a meaningful life. Success can be measured in different ways, depending on one's values and aspirations. For some, success may be reaching a high position in their career, starting and growing a successful business, or making a significant impact on society. For others, it could be maintaining strong relationships, being a loving parent, or pursuing personal passions and hobbies.
